Dogs

Money can buy you a fine dog, but only love can make him wag his tail. ...

Dogs are considered as one of my favourite pets. Not a single day passes without me playing and fondling with my pet dog, Sheroo. He is my best friend, and I do love him the most. But not only did he choose me as his friend, he has even become friends with the street dogs of our colony, as a result, I have made even more friends. I do love all of them from the core of my heart as they are the best part of my family. Thanks Sheroo, I dedicate this webpage to you, for being the most awesome part of my life! You owe me one :)

Cats

Cats are intended to teach us that not everything in nature has a function!!

Cats!!! Well cats were once a nuisance for me, but nowadays they too are finding a place on my friend list. And all these happened with the coming of my best friend Sheroo. But that was an irony though, since the day he made my home his home, the first friends he made were the neighbourhood cats, for he found those meow meows! to be a familiar cacophony is a "still going to be familiar" land. So, I too somehow had to listen to his silent request and befriend with the cats. Believe me, that was the coolest decision I made, and now I am a much more closer friend to them than Sheroo. I owe you one :)

Plants

Don't judge each day by the harvest you reap but by the seeds that you plant!!

We have a flower garden at our home. Once there was a lawn in the side of our home. It was a vast space which was left just like that other than parking. I planned myself and laid a flower garden of my own. I have all seasonal flowers in my garden. since I water them I take care of them like my little ones. I love to look at the flowers that are fresh after a new bloom. Every day as soon as I wake up I go to my garden and get some fresh air. The floral smell that enters inside me while I inhale is such a nice experience.
